NJIT Information & Cybersecurity Club (NICC)

The NJIT Information & Cybersecurity Club began with two students: Alfred Simpson and Jordani Jean-Pierre. Sharing a mutual interest in cybersecurity, the pair sought to increase awareness on campus. Quickly the club grew to a dozen members within the first few months, to 285 by their second year.

The Vector Newspaper

The Vector is entering it's 100th year! The Vector is older than NJIT itself. In its 100 years it has been entirely student run. It relies heavily on student participation, advertising, and external help to operate. The Vector gives students the opportunity to learn what it takes to bring the news to the world.

(ISC)²

(ISC)² envisions a secure future for the entire world. As a member of the student chapter, we help connect student members with extra resources, mentorship, and training to further their careers. Many know (ISC)² for their numerous certifications offered to verify the knowledge needed in the industry.

WJTB Radio

WJTB Radio is NJIT's official radio station. It got started in the 1960s and is entirely student run. At one point the radio station broadcasted on the AM radio. Today, WJTB broadcasts entirely online.
